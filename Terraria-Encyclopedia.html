JANUARY 2011
THE SPARK OF CREATION
Andrew "Redigit" Spinks, a programmer from Indiana, begins developing a 2D sandbox game from his living room. Inspired by Minecraft and his own previous work on the fan-game Super Mario Bros. X, he sets out to create something entirely new. A small remote team helps with art and testing.
MAY 16, 2011
TERRARIA LAUNCHES ON STEAM
Terraria releases on Steam for Windows — earlier than planned after a beta build was leaked online. The game sells over 50,000 copies on day one and 432,000 in its first month alone. Minecraft creator Markus "Notch" Persson tweets about the game, giving it a massive early boost.
v1.0STEAM LAUNCH
FEBRUARY 2012
DEVELOPMENT PAUSES
Redigit announces Terraria will receive no further content updates so he can focus on his family. Fans are disappointed, but a final bug-fix patch is released. The game continues selling strongly on Steam despite the halt in active development.
OCTOBER 2013
THE GREAT RETURN — VERSION 1.2
After a community outcry and fan support, Redigit returns to Terraria development. Version 1.2 launches with an enormous amount of new content — new biomes, the Crimson, hundreds of new items, NPCs, and enemies. This is widely considered the update that transformed Terraria from a good game into a legendary one. Terraria 2 is also hinted at.
v1.2CRIMSON BIOMEHUNDREDS OF ITEMS
2013–2014
CONSOLE & MOBILE EXPANSION
Re-Logic partners with Engine Software and 505 Games to bring Terraria to Xbox 360, PlayStation 3, and PS Vita. Mobile versions arrive for iOS and Android via Codeglue. PS4 and Xbox One versions come in 2014. Terraria becomes truly multiplatform. The Japanese console version features exclusive anime crossover content by Spike Chunsoft.
XBOX 360PS3VITAMOBILE
JUNE 2015
VERSION 1.3 — HARDMODE REBORN
Version 1.3 launches and adds an Expert Mode that dramatically increases the game's challenge, the Lunar Events, the Moon Lord final boss, the Celestial Pillars, and a new endgame tier of gear. tModLoader begins gaining traction, allowing modders to build massive content expansions. The Terraria community explodes with activity.
v1.3MOON LORDEXPERT MODE
2018–2019
TERRARIA: OTHERWORLD CANCELED
Terraria: Otherworld, a spin-off featuring tower defense and RPG elements, had been in development since February 2015. After working with Engine Software and then Pipeworks Studios, Re-Logic announces the project is canceled on April 12, 2018, as it didn't meet their quality standards. Resources return to the main game.
MAY 16, 2020
JOURNEY'S END — VERSION 1.4
Terraria's "final update" — released exactly 9 years after the game's original launch — is a monumental content drop. Journey Mode, Master Mode, Golf mini-game, hundreds of new items, bestiary, shimmer liquid, and much more. tModLoader receives official Steam support. Despite being announced as the last update, Re-Logic continues developing the game due to overwhelming demand.
v1.4JOURNEY'S ENDTMODLOADER OFFICIAL
2021
THE GOOGLE STADIA SAGA
Redigit's Google account is suspended without explanation, causing Re-Logic to cancel the Stadia port in protest. The story goes viral across gaming media. Google eventually apologizes and restores the account, and Re-Logic follows through with the Stadia release in March 2021 — honoring their commitment to players even after the drama.
2023–2025
STILL GROWING — VERSION 1.4.5
Re-Logic speaks out against Unity's controversial runtime fee policy in 2023, donating $100,000 to open-source alternatives Godot and FNA. Terraria crosses 64 million total copies sold by May 2025 — one of the best-selling games in history. The Calamity mod's soundtrack ranks 9th on Opera GX's all-time list. Re-Logic hires a Minecraft modder specifically to work on tModLoader.

👑 THE CREATORS & TEAM

🏗
⛏
ANDREW SPINKS
"Redigit" / Re-Logic President
FOUNDER & LEAD DEVELOPER
The genius behind Terraria. Working from his living room in Indiana, Redigit created the game almost single-handedly from January to May 2011. A former Nintendo fan-game developer (SMBX), he built Terraria using Microsoft's XNA framework. Known for his passion for the community and his "final update" meme-worthy announcements. His battles with Google over a suspended account made international gaming news.
🎨
WHITNEY SPINKS
"Cenx" — Vice President
VICE PRESIDENT & GAME DESIGNER
Wife of Redigit and essential creative force at Re-Logic. Whitney "Cenx" Spinks serves as Vice President and Game Designer, contributing significantly to Terraria's design direction and creative vision. She's been outspoken on matters of company ethics, including the statement that Re-Logic would never sell out to the Epic Games Store exclusivity deals.
🖌
FINN BRICE
"Tiyuri" — Original Sprite Artist
CO-FOUNDER (EARLY TEAM)
The original sprite artist of Terraria, Finn "Tiy" Brice was commissioned to handle the game's early graphics design. He departed the team in early 2012. After Terraria, he went on to found Chucklefish Games — the studio behind beloved indie titles like Starbound, Wargroove, and the publisher of Stardew Valley. A true indie legend.
🎵
SCOTT LLOYD SHELLY
"Resonance Array"
ORIGINAL COMPOSER
Scott Lloyd Shelly composed the haunting, atmospheric soundtrack for the original Terraria — tracks like "Overworld Day," "Boss 1," and "Underground" became iconic. The music set a tone that perfectly matched Terraria's blend of wonder and danger. His work is now part of gaming music history, even inspiring the massive Calamity mod soundtrack which outranked the original in community polls.
💻
YORAI OMER
"Yoraiz0r" — Lead Developer
PROGRAMMER (SINCE v1.2.3)
Hired alongside Solsund on January 6, 2014, Yoraiz0r became one of Terraria's Lead Developers. Previously a well-known modder on the official Terraria Online forums, he brought deep community knowledge and technical expertise to the team. He was instrumental in the massive 1.3 and 1.4 updates.
📢
TED MURPHY
"Loki" — Head of Marketing
HEAD OF BUSINESS STRATEGY
Ted "Loki" Murphy was originally hired as Re-Logic's Community Manager during development of update 1.3. Previously a moderator on Terraria Online, he brought community-management skills to an official role. He is now the Head of Business Strategy & Marketing, known for his community-friendly communication style and detailed dev posts.
🎮
VICTOR MOURA
"Crowno" — Lead Artist
LEAD ARTIST & SPRITE DESIGNER
Victor "Crowno" Moura serves as Terraria's primary sprite artist and Lead Artist at Re-Logic. His pixel art brought the game's iconic look to life — from the whimsical NPCs to terrifying bosses. His detailed sprite work on post-1.2 Terraria helped define the game's visual identity that millions recognize today.
🏃
AMANDA POWELL
"Safeman" — Community Manager
COMMUNITY MANAGER
Amanda "Safeman" Powell is Terraria's Community Manager and admin, handling communications and player relations. Taking over from Loki in November 2015, she has been the friendly face of Re-Logic's forums and social media presence. Her warm, engaged management style helped maintain one of gaming's most loyal fanbases through multiple "final update" cycles.

📜 THE LORE OF TERRARIA

🌒
THE OFFICIAL LORE — 8TH ANNIVERSARY RELEASE
On May 16, 2019 — Terraria's 8th anniversary — Re-Logic released official lore written on inked parchment images. While initially released without a clear story, this document connected the game's characters and gameplay elements into a coherent universe. The developers noted this is one possible interpretation, and players are encouraged to piece together their own stories.
⚖ THE DIVINE BALANCE
In the beginning, the Gods established a balance to guarantee fairness for all living creatures. This balance was of paramount importance. Eons have passed since the Gods began testing the complexities of life. Each world of Terraria is a sentient being that knows all that happens within it — its sole purpose being to ensure the desired balance is maintained, often violently. The worlds utilize defense mechanisms: the Hallow, Corruption, and Crimson.
🦑 THE COMING OF CTHULHU
Your story begins with Cthulhu — a creature of immeasurable power and unknown origin — who arrived long ago with the sole purpose of raining destruction and having dominion over all sentient life. None could stand against Cthulhu. The very fabric of Terraria seemed on the precipice of doom. At last, the ancient race of Dryads arose to wage battle against this cosmic horror.
🌿 THE DRYADS' SACRIFICE
The Dryads, with their unparalleled connection to the planet, were Terraria's last hope. They joined together to save all life from annihilation. The Dryads could not kill Cthulhu — but with their combined power, they crippled Cthulhu's ability to cause further damage by ripping out his eyes, part of his skeleton, and chunks of his brain. This forced Cthulhu to retreat to the dark side of the moon, where it dwells to this day.
🧟 THE CORRUPTION & CRIMSON
The Crimson is a single emergent living being connected directly to each world, sharing a hive mind, solely focused on restoring balance. The Corruption serves a similar purpose — but manifests differently, as a spreading darkness that consumes and transforms. Both are the world's immune system, reacting to the presence of too much good or evil — the eternal struggle that every player lives through.
⚔ THE GUIDE & THE PLAYER
The player character — the "Terrarian" — arrives in this world as a fresh adventurer. The Guide NPC, who spawns at the start, is the first friendly face and provides the clues needed to progress. Lore hints suggest the Guide has a deeper connection to the world than it first appears. His mysterious death at the Wall of Flesh's summoning is no coincidence — it's key to unlocking Hardmode.
👁 THE WALL OF FLESH
Deep in the Underworld, the Wall of Flesh serves as the ultimate guardian of the pre-Hardmode world — a gate between two eras of power. Sacrificing the Guide NPC in a pool of lava summons this terrifying boss. Defeating it "awakens" the world's dormant mechanical evils and transforms it forever, corrupting previously safe areas and spawning entirely new challenges.

💀 LEGENDARY BOSSES

👹
👁
KING SLIME
PRE-HARDMODE | OPTIONAL
The first boss most players encounter. A massive royal blue slime that splits into smaller slimes as it takes damage. Drops the Ninja set vanity and the Slime Staff accessory.
HP: 2,000
👁
EYE OF CTHULHU
PRE-HARDMODE | FIRST BOSS
One of the first bosses, summoned at night or by a Suspicious Looking Eye. Phase 1: charges with servants. Phase 2: transforms into a mouth and becomes far more aggressive. A cornerstone of early Terraria progression.
HP: 2,800
🕷
EATER OF WORLDS
PRE-HARDMODE | CORRUPTION
A massive segmented worm boss found in Corruption worlds. Each segment has individual HP and splitting the worm creates two smaller worms. Defeating it provides Demonite Ore and Shadow Scales for crafting powerful pre-Hardmode gear.
HP: 7,485
🧠
BRAIN OF CTHULHU
PRE-HARDMODE | CRIMSON
The Crimson world's alternative to the Eater of Worlds. A horrifying floating brain surrounded by Creepers. Phase 2 reveals the brain itself after all Creepers are destroyed, teleporting rapidly. Provides Crimtane Ore and Tissue Samples.
HP: 2,000
👧
QUEEN BEE
PRE-HARDMODE | JUNGLE
The guardian of the Underground Jungle's larva. Queen Bee charges, fires stingers, and summons bees. Defeating her yields Bee Wax and the Abeemination. One of the most rewarding pre-Hardmode bosses for ranged class players.
HP: 3,400
💀
SKELETRON
PRE-HARDMODE | DUNGEON GUARDIAN
The guardian of the Dungeon. Skeletron is a skull with spinning arms that must all be defeated in the same night. Failing to kill it before sunrise results in massive punishment. Defeating him unlocks the Dungeon's treasures and the Clothier NPC.
HP: 4,400
👄
WALL OF FLESH
PRE-HARDMODE | FINAL BOSS
The pre-Hardmode final boss and the gateway to the game's second stage. A massive wall of flesh that spans the entire Underworld, complete with eyes, mouths, and the Hungry. Defeating it permanently transforms the world into Hardmode — the point of no return.
HP: 8,000
🤖
THE TWINS
HARDMODE | MECHANICAL
Two mechanical eyes — Retinazer (laser) and Spazmatism (cursed flames) — that must both be defeated in the same night. One of three mechanical bosses that must be slain to progress through Hardmode. Drops Hallowed Bars and Souls of Sight.
HP: 43,000 (combined)
🦴
SKELETRON PRIME
HARDMODE | MECHANICAL
The mechanical counterpart of Skeletron, armed with mechanical arms including a Vice, Saw, Cannon, and Laser. Must be defeated before dawn or it becomes invincible. Drops Hallowed Bars and Souls of Fright.
HP: 28,000+
⚙
THE DESTROYER
HARDMODE | MECHANICAL WORM
A massive mechanical version of the Eater of Worlds, with Probes shooting lasers attached to its body. One of the first Hardmode bosses most players tackle, as it drops both Hallowed Bars and Souls of Might needed for Hardmode armor progression.
HP: 80,000
🌿
PLANTERA
HARDMODE | JUNGLE GUARDIAN
The guardian of the Underground Jungle, awakened by destroying her Bulb. Phase 1 is a large flower that chases you with thorned hooks. Phase 2 transforms into a horrifying spiky mouth. Defeating Plantera is a major milestone that unlocks the Temple and the full Dungeon in Hardmode.
HP: 30,000
🌸
EMPRESS OF LIGHT
HARDMODE | HALLOW GUARDIAN
One of Terraria's most visually stunning bosses, the Empress of Light attacks with rainbow-colored beams of prismatic light. Defeating her during daytime — without taking a single hit — is considered the ultimate Terraria skill challenge and grants the Terraprisma summon weapon.
HP: 70,000
👁
DUKE FISHRON
HARDMODE | OPTIONAL
A flying pig-shark-dragon hybrid found by fishing in the Ocean biome with Truffle Worm bait. One of the hardest optional bosses with devastating charge attacks. His drops include the Tempest Staff, Tsunami, and Fishron Wings — some of the best gear available before the endgame.
HP: 50,000
🏛
GOLEM
HARDMODE | TEMPLE GUARDIAN
The guardian of the Jungle Temple, summoned with Lihzahrd Power Cells at the Altar. Golem attacks with fireballs, fist projectiles, and an exposed head in Phase 2. Defeating Golem opens the path to the final questline of Terraria's main story arc.
HP: 9,000
🧙
LUNATIC CULTIST
POST-GOLEM | CELESTIAL GATE
The cloaked mastermind who summons the Lunar Events. Found at the Dungeon with his cultist followers after defeating Golem. His defeat triggers the final battle phase of vanilla Terraria — the Celestial Pillars and the approach of the Moon Lord.
HP: 32,000
🌙
MOON LORD
POST-GOLEM | FINAL BOSS
The final boss of Terraria's vanilla game and Cthulhu's true form. Three eyes in his hands and forehead, plus a true eye in his core once the outer eyes are defeated. Moon Lord has some of the highest HP in the game and drops the most powerful vanilla weapons including Terrarian, Meowmere, and S.D.M.G.
HP: 145,000

🌍 BIOMES & DIMENSIONS

🗺
🌲
FOREST
The starting biome. Green trees, slimes, and the Guide NPC. The safest place in the world — for now. Home to the surface and early resources.
🏜
DESERT
Sandy wasteland with Antlion enemies. Underground Desert features ancient fossils, sandstone blocks, and the formidable Desert Tiger Staff.
❄
SNOW BIOME
A frozen tundra with ice blocks and unique enemies. Underground Ice biome yields the Staff of Frost, Blizzard Staff, and Frost armor in Hardmode.
🌿
JUNGLE
The most dangerous early biome. Dense vegetation, Hornets, and Man Eaters. Underground Jungle hides Plantera Bulbs, Life Fruit, and the Jungle Temple.
🌊
OCEAN
Found at world edges. Sharks, Crabs, and Starfish. The Ocean contains Water Chests and is the only place to find Duke Fishron via deep-sea fishing.
💜
CORRUPTION
One of two world evils. Purple spreading biome with Chasms, Devourers, and the Eater of Worlds boss. Contains Demonite Ore and Shadow Chests.
🩸
CRIMSON
The alternative world evil. Red grotesque terrain filled with Face Monsters and the Brain of Cthulhu. Contains Crimtane Ore and Ichor in Hardmode.
✨
THE HALLOW
A "good" biome born in Hardmode. Rainbow trees, Unicorns, and Pixies. Source of Holy Arrows, Blessed Apples, and the Empress of Light boss arena.
🔥
THE UNDERWORLD
The deepest layer of the world, a hellish landscape of Hellstone, lava lakes, and Ruined Houses. Home of the Wall of Flesh and the most valuable pre-HM ores.
🏰
THE DUNGEON
An underground fortress guarded by Skeletron. Contains powerful chests with Muramasa, Cobalt Shield, and in Hardmode — Dungeon Spirits and Biome Chests.
☁
SPACE / SKY
The uppermost layer of the world. Floating islands hide Skyware Chests with Starfury, Lucky Horseshoe, and Shiny Red Balloons. Harpies patrol the skies.
🍄
MUSHROOM BIOME
A glowing blue underground paradise with giant mushrooms and Truffle NPCs. Surface Glowing Mushroom biomes host the Truffle NPC and provide the only Truffle Worm — essential for Duke Fishron.

📦 MAJOR UPDATES

🔄
v1.0
THE ORIGINAL RELEASE
May 16, 2011
The game that started it all. 6 bosses, basic biomes, and the core loop of dig-build-fight that would define a genre. Sold 50,000 copies on day one. Built on Microsoft XNA in just a few months of frenzied development.
v1.1
HARDMODE AWAKENING
December 1, 2011
Added Hardmode — the game-within-a-game that begins after defeating the Wall of Flesh. Mechanical bosses, Hallowed biome, new ore tiers, and vastly increased difficulty. Transformed Terraria from a good game into a deep one.
v1.2
THE GREAT EXPANSION
October 1, 2013
Redigit's legendary return. Added the Crimson biome as an alternative to Corruption, hundreds of new items, the Dungeon's post-Plantera content, Ocean biome enemies, new NPCs, and the Solar Eclipse event. Widely considered the best update in Terraria history.
v1.3
LUNAR APOCALYPSE
June 30, 2015
Added Expert Mode, the Lunar Events, Celestial Pillars, the Moon Lord final boss, a new endgame tier, and tModLoader support laying the ground for the mod community. Also added Duke Fishron and the Goblin Tinkerer's reforge system revamp.
v1.3.5
QUALITY IMPROVEMENTS
September 26, 2017
Added a new NPC (Tax Collector), the Old One's Army event crossover with Dungeon Defenders, new items, and improved world generation. Set the stage for the final chapter of Terraria.
v1.4
JOURNEY'S END
May 16, 2020
The "final" update released on Terraria's 9th birthday. Journey Mode, Master Mode, Golf, Bestiary, Shimmer liquid, Empress of Light, Queen Slime, hundreds of new items, and tModLoader on Steam. The community's "final update" meme was born.
v1.4.1–1.4.3
LABOR OF LOVE
2021–2022
Despite "Journey's End," Re-Logic kept updating. Added Don't Starve Together crossover content, resource pack support via Steam Workshop, and massive quality-of-life improvements. The community grew even larger.
v1.4.4
DON'T STARVE CROSSOVER
September 28, 2022
A surprise full content update adding the Deerclops boss from Don't Starve Together, new items, biome torches improvements, the Terraprisma challenge, new town NPC interactions, and the controversial Shimmer liquid that converts items.
v1.4.5
CROSSOVER EXTRAVAGANZA
2024
Added cross-game collaborations, hundreds of balance changes, new vanity sets, improved worldgen, the Dead Cells crossover content, and quality-of-life refinements across the board. Proof that Re-Logic would never truly stop developing Terraria.

🧪 THE MODDING UNIVERSE

⚗
Terraria's modding scene is one of the most vibrant in all of gaming. Powered by tModLoader — which received official Steam support in 2020 — thousands of mods ranging from small quality-of-life tweaks to massive content expansions have been created. In 2022, Re-Logic even hired a Minecraft modder specifically to work on tModLoader. The Calamity mod's soundtrack even outranked the base game in a 2024 Opera GX poll.
CALAMITY MOD
MASSIVE CONTENT EXPANSION
The most downloaded Terraria mod of all time. Adds an enormous number of new bosses (24+), items, biomes, and a complete post-Moon Lord progression system. The Calamity soundtrack ranked 9th among all video game soundtracks ever in a 2024 poll — beating the base game itself. Developed by MountainDrew/Fabsol.
📥 Downloads: 16M+ on tModLoader
THORIUM MOD
CLASS & CONTENT EXPANSION
Adds three entirely new classes — Healer, Bard, and Thrower — plus hundreds of items, biomes, and bosses that feel as polished as vanilla Terraria. Thorium is often cited as the most "vanilla-feeling" large content mod, making it ideal for first-time modded playthroughs.
📥 Downloads: 8M+ on tModLoader
SPIRIT MOD
CONTENT EXPANSION
Adds new biomes, bosses, enemies, and items with unique lore. The Spirit Mod is known for its high-quality sprites and its Briar and Ancient biomes. A beloved entry in the modding community for players who want more lore depth alongside their new content.
📥 Downloads: 4M+ on tModLoader
STARS ABOVE
STORY & CHARACTERS
A unique mod that adds story-driven characters, dialogue, and a narrative layer to Terraria's world. Features deep lore integration, unique weapons called "Aspected Weapons," and a series of questline-style events. A fan favorite for players who want a more RPG-like experience.
📥 Downloads: 2M+ on tModLoader
TMODLOADER
MOD FRAMEWORK (OFFICIAL)
The backbone of all Terraria modding. tModLoader allows players to install, manage, and play with mods directly through Steam. After receiving official Re-Logic support in 2020 and its own Steam page, it has become the definitive way to experience Terraria's massive modding ecosystem.
📥 1M+ Steam installs
OVERHAUL
GAMEPLAY OVERHAUL
Transforms Terraria's gameplay with seasonal changes, rolling dodges, new sound design, improved combat mechanics, and dynamic lighting. Terraria Overhaul aims to modernize Terraria's mechanics for a fresh experience while preserving the game's core identity — a controversial but acclaimed redesign.
📥 Downloads: 3M+ on tModLoader
